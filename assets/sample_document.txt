ACME Corporation Employee Handbook
==================================

Chapter 1: Vacation Policy
--------------------------
All full-time employees are entitled to paid time off (PTO) based on their tenure:
- 0-2 years: 15 days per year
- 2-5 years: 20 days per year
- 5+ years: 25 days per year

Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.
Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

Chapter 2: Remote Work Policy
-----------------------------
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM in your local timezone.
All remote work must be conducted from a secure, private location.
VPN connection is required when accessing company resources remotely.

Chapter 3: Expense Reimbursement
--------------------------------
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.
Receipts are required for all expenses over $25.
Pre-approval is required for expenses exceeding $500.
Travel expenses include: airfare (economy class), hotels (up to $200/night), and meals (up to $75/day).

Chapter 4: Professional Development
-----------------------------------
Each employee has an annual learning budget of $2,000.
Approved uses include: conferences, online courses, books, and certifications.
Time off for professional development must be coordinated with your manager.
Employees are encouraged to share learnings with their teams through presentations or documentation.

Chapter 5: Health and Wellness
------------------------------
The company provides comprehensive health insurance for all full-time employees.
Dental and vision coverage is included at no additional cost.
A gym membership reimbursement of up to $50/month is available.
Mental health resources include access to counseling services through our EAP program.
